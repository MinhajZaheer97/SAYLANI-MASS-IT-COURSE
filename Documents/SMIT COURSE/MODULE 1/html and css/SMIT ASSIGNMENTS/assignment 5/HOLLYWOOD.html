**Hollywood** is the name of a neighborhood in **Los Angeles, California**, that has become synonymous with the **American film industry**. Globally recognized as the historic center of **movie and television production**, Hollywood began rising to prominence in the early 20th century, with major studios like Paramount, Warner Bros., Universal, and others establishing their roots there. Over time, the name "Hollywood" came to represent not just a physical location, but the entire U.S. entertainment industry, especially the commercial film sector known for big-budget productions, star-driven marketing, and global influence. Hollywood is known for producing a wide range of films across genres, often characterized by high production values, advanced technology, and wide international distribution. It has played a key role in shaping global pop culture and remains a symbol of fame, glamour, and cinematic excellence.
