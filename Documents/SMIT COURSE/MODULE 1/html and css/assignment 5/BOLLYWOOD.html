Bollywood is the informal name for the Hindi-language film industry based in Mumbai, India, and is one of the largest and most influential film industries in the world. Originating in the early 20th century and gaining global recognition by the 1970s, Bollywood is known for its vibrant storytelling that blends genres like drama, romance, action, and comedy, often featuring elaborate song-and-dance sequences. Unlike Hollywood, Bollywood films are typically longer and heavily influenced by Indian culture, values, and traditions. It plays a significant role in shaping Indian popular culture and has a massive following both domestically and internationally.
