Rock Paper Scissors

Rock Paper Scissors is a fast-paced strategy game where players compete against an AI opponent by selecting one of three classic moves: Rock, Paper, or Scissors. Every round is decided instantly according to the traditional rules of the game.

This single-player edition is designed to provide quick entertainment with responsive controls, smooth gameplay, and realistic sound effects. The AI generates its move randomly, ensuring that every match remains fair and unpredictable.

Genre: Strategy / Casual Game

Game Mode: Single Player vs AI

Opponent: Computer AI

Objective: Defeat the AI by choosing the winning move according to the classic Rock Paper Scissors rules.

Winning Rules: Rock defeats Scissors, Scissors defeat Paper, and Paper defeats Rock.

Features: Random AI moves, score tracking, winner announcement, sound effects, interactive controls, new game option, and responsive gameplay.

Technology: HTML, CSS and JavaScript

Developer: Minhaj

Status: Ready to Play


This implementation delivers the classic Rock Paper Scissors experience in a modern browser environment. With instant AI responses, live scorekeeping, and an intuitive interface, players can enjoy quick matches anytime without requiring installation or additional software.
